About RoadWatch

RoadWatch is an intelligent AI-powered road monitoring web application designed to improve road safety and infrastructure maintenance.

The application helps users detect potholes, road cracks, damaged roads and other road-related issues by uploading road images through the system.

RoadWatch also empowers citizens by providing a platform to monitor road quality and track public expenditure on infrastructure projects.

Users can upload images, manage complaints, view reports and monitor road condition statistics through a smart dashboard system.

This project was developed by a team of engineering students as part of an innovative smart transportation and public safety initiative.
